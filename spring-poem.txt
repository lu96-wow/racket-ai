Spring Morning

The morning dew glistens on petals so bright,
A gentle breeze whispers through soft, golden light.
The robin sings sweetly from the budding tree,
Awakening the world in harmony.

Tulips and daffodils dance in the breeze,
Their colors a painting among the green trees.
The fragrance of blossoms fills the warm air,
A promise of beauty beyond compare.

Oh, spring, sweet spring, you've come again,
To wash away the winter's pain.
With every bloom and sunlit ray,
You breathe new life into the day.
